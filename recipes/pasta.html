Pasta Salad

description

Pasta salad is a delightful and versatile dish that combines cooked padta with a medley of freash vegetables, herbs, and often a tangy dressing. It's a popular cold salad, perfect for picnics, barbecues, and potlucks. The pasta provides a hearty base, while the ingredients add color, flavor, and texture.

Dressings can vary, from classic vinaigrettes to creamy options like ranch or mayo-based dressings. Pasta salad offers a refreshing and satisfying combination of flavor and is customizable to suit various preferences, making it a favorite summertime side dish. whether you prefer a zesty italian pasta salad or a creamy, indulgent variation, this dish is a crowd-pleaser that brings a burst of freshness to any meal or gathering

Ingredients

  • 1 pound tri-colored spiral pasta
  • 1 (16 ounce) bottle Italian-style salad dressing
  • 6 tablespoons salad seasoning mix
  • 2 cups cherry tomatoes, diced
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1/2 yellow bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 (2.25 ounce) can black olives, chopped

Steps

  1. Gtaher all ingredients
  2. cook pasta in salted water
  3. whisk Italian dressing and salad spice mix together until smooth
  4. Combine pasta, tomatoes, bell peppers, and olives ina salad bowl
  5. Pour dressing over salad and toss to coat
  6. Refrigerate salad, 8 hours to overnight
  7. Enjoy!
